FAQs

How does URL shortening work?

URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, condensed version that redirects to the original URL. When a user clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the intended destination.

Is it necessary to create an account to use the URL shortening service?

No, you can shorten links without an account. However, creating an account gives you access to advanced features like link management, customization, and tracking analytics.

Are the shortened links permanent? Will they expire?

By default, shortened links do not expire and remain active as long as our service is running. In some cases, custom or promotional links may have expiration dates, but you’ll always be notified when that applies.

Are there any limitations on the number of URLs I can shorten?

Free users can shorten as many links as they like, but heavy usage may be subject to fair use limits. Premium accounts offer higher or unlimited link shortening capacity.

Can I customize the shortened URLs to reflect my brand or content?

Yes! You can create branded or custom aliases for your links. Instead of a random string, your link could look like yourbrand.com/offer for better recognition and trust.

Can I track the performance of my shortened URLs?

Absolutely. With an account, you can see detailed analytics including click counts, geographic location of visitors, device type, and referral sources.

How secure is the URL shortening service? Are the shortened links protected against spam or malicious activity?

Our system uses advanced filters and checks to block spam, phishing, or harmful content. Suspicious links are automatically disabled to protect users from malicious activity.

What is a QR code and what can it do?

A QR code is a scannable barcode that can instantly open your shortened link when scanned with a phone camera. This makes it easy to share URLs offline, such as on posters, flyers, or packaging.

Is there an API available for integrating the URL shortening service into my own applications or websites?

Yes, we provide a REST API that allows developers to generate, manage, and track shortened links programmatically. You can integrate it into apps, websites, or marketing tools.
